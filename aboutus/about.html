ABOUT US


Bukkusenta Online Bookcenter is a commercial online bookstore platform.
The name is inspired by Japanese translation for “bookcenter”. Focused on offering a variety of fiction and non - fiction books through digital platforms. The items are available in Malay and English varies for over total number of books.
Clients or customers are able to purchase new items offered.
We also offer our beloved customers with used or secondhand items.
Those secondhand items offered are readable and considered in good condition without much damage.
The used books are sold for a lower price compared to the original price which is affordable for all people to get reading materials.we welcomes all kinds of audience as its customers to browse the items provided especially for avid readers.
