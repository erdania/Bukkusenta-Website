PRODUCT AND SERVICES

Beside sell books to the clients, is there any other services offers?
  • Bukkusenta Online Bookcenter Sdn Bhd also offer clients with used / secondhand refurbished items at the lower price compared to the new one.

If I received a damaged or incorrect item, can I return it?
  • According to our Return & Refund Policy(link), clients can return the item and will get full refund if clients refuse to continue purchasing. However, we regret that the shipping charges will not be refunds. The items must be returned in its original forms and condition including the packaging and accessories received.
What about “used books” section bout and do the items are in good condition?
  • The used books sections are our speciality in providing clients with affordable items instead of new items. The items are in good condition and readable since we will be refurbished it first before publishing in our websites.

DELIVERY

Does Bukkusenta Online Bookcenter ships books internationally?
  • Bukkusenta Online Bookcenter does not ship items internationally. For the time being, we only ship items within Malaysia only.
How many charges per delivery?
  • For shipping within Peninsular Malaysia, the order above RM100 is provided with free shipping. For order below RM100, the shipping cost will be based on the items weight and location for delivery.
How long does it take for orders to be processed and shipped?
  • The orders will be processed within 24 hours from the time of payments is confirmed. The orders after 5.00 pm is accepted but will be processed on the next day (business days). An email will be sent to clients once order is process. Order will be shipped within 3 – 5 days of business days depend on the availability of items whether it is in – stock or out – of – stock.
What couriers shipped out items used by Bukkusenta? Can I choose the couriers?
  • For the time being, we use DHL, POS LAJU and J&T services. The service for ships will be determine by us as some location cannot be reach by certain services. Sorry for the inconvenient and we hope clients can understand.
